Project Explanation

The chest I based the model off is from an old treasure chest playset my parents bought me back in elementary school. High quality stuff, the only thing thats plastic is the skull. Everything else is wood, paint, and screws. Since the screws blend in with the wood I used a bump map for them, and I used one on the skull to get the assorted face holes without complicating the geometry.

The yellow thing is called Pompompurin. I made him for a little art piece I got signed during my study abroad. I'd show a picture but I don't have it on me.
